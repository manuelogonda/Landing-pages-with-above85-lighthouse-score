The Experience

Where African stories
meet fine craft.

Ember was built on a single belief: Nairobi deserves a restaurant and hotel that is truly its own. Every dish draws from Kenyan and East African heritage — reimagined with modern technique and integrity.

  • Executive chef with 15 years of fine dining experience
  • Over 80% ingredients sourced within Kenya
  • Private dining room for events up to 40 guests
  • Rooftop bar open Thursday to Sunday 5PM–midnight
  • Complimentary breakfast included with all rooms
